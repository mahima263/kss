Kotak Mahindra Bank & IIT Kanpur Partnership

The Kotak School of Sustainability at IIT Kanpur aims to excel in sustainability education, research and innovation, technology development, entrepreneurship, and outreach. The school will spearhead the development of holistic end-to-end technology solutions for sustainable development for a healthy planet and species.

Sustainability Education, Research & Outreach

Cohesive integration of the underlying themes of sustainability for greater educational,scientific, technological, environmental and social impact.

Our Vision
